Welcome to the Randomizer App!

This app provides various tools to help you make random selections and roll dice for your games or decision-making needs.

Whether you need to choose a random item from a list, roll different types of dice, or get quick descriptions, this app has got you covered. Explore the components below to get started!

Oracle

The Oracle component is designed to provide quick and random answers to your questions. It can be used for decision-making, storytelling, or simply for fun. Click the "Roll" button to get a random response from the predefined list of options.

Descriptions

The Description components provide random short and long descriptions that can be used for characters, settings, or items in your stories or games. Click the "Choose" button to get a random description from the predefined lists.

Dice Roller

The Dice Roller components allow you to roll various types of dice commonly used in tabletop games. Simply click the button corresponding to the die you want to roll, and the result will be displayed.
